# Godkiller

Godkiller's Lullaby is Ken's mythpunk/cyberpunk fiction world.

## Tone

Preserve a tone that is:

- intense
- poetic
- brutal
- cinematic
- emotionally charged
- mythic and technologically haunted

Do not include large private manuscript content in this public repo.

Do not include sensitive unpublished details beyond high-level guidance unless Ken explicitly moves them here.
